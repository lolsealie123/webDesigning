Link States

Links have multiple states depending on the situation. Each state has its own selector. They are:

  • a:link - an unvisited link
  • a:visited - a link that has been visited before
  • a:hover - the link when the mouse is over it
  • a:active - a link that is currently selected

IMPORTANT: The selectors for link states MUST be in a certain order. a:hover MUST come after a:link and a:visited. a:active MUST come after a:hover.
